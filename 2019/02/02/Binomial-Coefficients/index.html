$\dbinom{n}{k} = \dbinom{n}{n-k}$ integer $n \geqslant 0$, integer k 5.4 symmetry
$\dbinom{r}{k} =\frac{r}{k} \dbinom{n-1}{k-1}$ integer $k \ne 0$ 5.5 absorption/extraction
$\dbinom{r}{k} = \dbinom{r-1}{k} + \dbinom{r-1}{k-1}$ integer k 5.8 addition/induction
$\dbinom{r}{k} = (-1)^k \dbinom{k-r-1}{k}$ integer k 5.14 upper negation
